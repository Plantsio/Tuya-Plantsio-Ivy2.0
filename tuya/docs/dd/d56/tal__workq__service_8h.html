enum WORKQ_SERVICE_E

TuyaOS provides developers with two workqueue service for convenience.

枚举值
WORKQ_SYSTEM 

low priority workqueue(block operations are allowed)

WORKQ_HIGHTPRI 

high priority workqueue (block operations are not allowed)
